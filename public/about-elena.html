About the Doctor

Dr. Elena was born in DuBois, Pennsylvania and is a graduate of Villanova University. She obtained her medical degree at Thomas Jefferson University in Philadelphia. Her residency was at Thomas Jefferson and its affiliated Wills Eye Hospital, and she completed her training with fellowships at the University of Connecticut in cataract and corneal surgery.
